How to play (MVP rules)

  • 4 players max: Red, Blue, Yellow, Green (auto-assigned in join order).
  • Roll a 6 to bring a piece out from your yard to the start. A 6 gives another roll.
  • Click a highlighted piece to move. Landing on an opponent (not on safe squares) sends them back to yard.
  • Exact roll is required to enter the final home cell. First to bring all 4 home wins.
